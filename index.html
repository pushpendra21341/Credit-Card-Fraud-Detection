What is Credit Card Fraud

Credit card fraud is the unauthorized use of credit or debit card information to make purchases or transactions without the cardholder's permission. It can happen through stolen cards, card skimming, phishing, identity theft, or account takeover. This type of fraud leads to financial losses for cardholders and issuers and undermines trust in the financial system. Effective fraud detection measures, including machine learning models, are crucial to detect and prevent such fraudulent activities.
