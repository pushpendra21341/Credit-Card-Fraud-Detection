We have developed a credit card fraud detection model using different techniques such as logistic regression, decision tree classifier, and random forest classifier. To address the highly imbalanced nature of the dataset, we applied undersampling and oversampling techniques. The model's objective is to accurately distinguish between genuine and fraudulent transactions based on historical transaction records.
